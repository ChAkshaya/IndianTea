Tea Making Process

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Milk
  • Tea Powder of your choice
  • Sugar
  • Optional addition of spices

How to Make Chai Tea at Home

  1. Boil water in a saucepan.
  2. Add tea powder, sugar, and spices (like cardamom or ginger) to the boiling water.
  3. Pour in milk and let it simmer.
  4. Strain the tea into cups and serve hot.
